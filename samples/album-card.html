About This Album

"Chromatic" is the third studio album by American singer-songwriter Ava Martinez, released on March 10, 2023. The album represents a bold artistic evolution for Martinez, blending her signature R&B vocals with innovative electronic production and vivid lyrical storytelling.

Recorded over two years in studios across Los Angeles, London, and Tokyo, "Chromatic" explores themes of love, identity, and self-discovery through a vibrant sonic palette. The album's title reflects its conceptual approach to color as a metaphor for emotional states and personal transformation.

Martinez collaborated with a diverse range of producers including Max Martin, Jack Antonoff, and herself (under her production alias "Spectrum") to create what critics have called "a masterclass in contemporary pop music." The album features guest appearances from rapper Jaden Cole on the remix of lead single "Neon Love" and British singer-songwriter James Blake on the haunting ballad "Silhouettes."

Upon release, "Chromatic" debuted at #1 in 32 countries and received widespread critical acclaim. Rolling Stone named it "the defining pop album of 2023" while Pitchfork praised its "daring experimentation balanced with irresistible pop sensibility." The album earned Martinez eight Grammy nominations, including Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Album.

The accompanying "Chromatic World Tour" broke numerous attendance records and was hailed for its innovative stage design featuring real-time visual projections that responded to the music's frequencies and Martinez's movements.
