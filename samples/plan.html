Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual payments. All transactions are securely processed through our payment gateway.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remaining period. Downgrades will take effect at your next billing cycle.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment?

No, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime, though we offer significant discounts for annual commitments.

How does the royalty payment process work?

We collect royalties from all platforms and pay out monthly. Payments are made around the 15th of each month for earnings from the previous month. Minimum payout is $50 for PayPal and $100 for bank transfers.
