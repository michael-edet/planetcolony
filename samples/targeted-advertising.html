Our Advertising Methodology

We follow a proven process to ensure every advertising dollar delivers maximum impact

1

Audience Analysis

We analyze streaming patterns, social behavior, and demographic data to build detailed listener profiles that inform our targeting strategy.

2

Creative Development

Our team creates platform-specific ad creatives tailored to each audience segment, testing multiple variations for optimal performance.

3

Strategic Placement

We identify the most effective ad placements and timing based on when your target audience is most engaged with music content.

4

Performance Optimization

Continuous monitoring and adjustment of campaigns based on real-time data to improve results throughout the campaign duration.

5

Conversion Tracking

We implement advanced tracking to measure not just clicks, but downstream actions like saves, playlist adds, and repeat listens.

6

Post-Campaign Analysis

Detailed reporting and insights to understand what worked and how to improve future campaigns.
